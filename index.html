Services

Interpreting

Offering Medical, Educational, and Business interpreting in Spanish.

On-site Interpreting

For on-site interpreting, the interpreter is actually present at the session. On-site interpreting is the most effective way of interpreting since it allows face-to-face contact between provider, the limited English proficient, and the interpreter.

Fit for your corporate needs

Assure the language needs of limited English Proficient (LEP) patient/client are met in accordance with Title VI of the Civil Rights Act in relation to the delivery of programs in Health and Human services.

Telephonic Interpreting

Telephonic Interpreting takes place when the provider needs an Interpreter to connect with a Limited English Proficient (LEP) individual over the phone, immediately or at a pre-scheduled time.

Translation

Translation is the written form of one language to another. Some examples are, but not limited to, patient forms, medical history records, academic transcripts, and immigration documents.

Spanish/English Lessons

Here at Smoky Mountain Language Services we want to help the Limited English Proficient (LEP) person become proficient in English. To help break down that communication barrier. Assisting students/parents with proper pronunciation, composition, grammar, and dialogue.
